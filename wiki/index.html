Get Started

Gens - Grind half of your life

In Gens there are 3 different worlds, where
you can mine regenerating blocks or kill
strong mobs. The inventory is in every world
the same. You get to each world using the
NPC which stands next to the spawn.

  • In the normal Gens world you mine regenerating blocks. You compress them at the villagers behind the spawn and buy then stronger armor. The strongest armor you can get in "Gens" is the "Extreme Armor". There is also a parkour with better gens and villagers to trade food, a villager for farming and a villager which trades nearly everything, e.g. a Mace, Double Health, Smithing Templates and much more.

  • At the Warden Gens you have to kill a very strong Warden, who gives you Warden Essences. The warden spawns every 90 seconds. With these Essences you can upgrade your tools to even stronger ones.

  • At the Sky Gens are also very strong mobs (Breeze, Ravager, Wither Skeleton), which you have to kill and with a chance of 20% you get a Sky Essence. The spawning time is random. With the Sky Essences you can buy the strongest tools on the server.

Lobby

Explore our big detailed lobby with its nice features!

  • Map

    We have a big detailed map as lobby. Complete the parkour or discover its secrets and find hidden places where you could get some nice stuff.

  • Lobby Cosmetics

    On your 2nd Hotbar slot is a beacon. If you right-click it, a menu will open where you can buy lobby cosmetics using Mystery Dust. You get Mystery Dust from Crates, which is explained below.

  • Crates

    On the right side of the spawn are crates. Everyday you log in and click on the NPC for the Daily Rewards you will get 1 Mystery Dust and 1 Ranks Crate. If you use the Mystery Dust Crate, you get 10-10000 Mystery Dust which you can use for the Lobby Cosmetics. If you use the Ranks Crate you could get ranks. The ranks are explained down below.

Ranks

Here we will give you informations about our player ranks.

VIP Rank

The VIP Rank is easy to get. It has a chance of 13% to get it in a crate and you can buy it in Gens for 2 Super Compressed Netherite. With the VIP Rank you can write with color codes in the chat (&4hi) and you can do /feed, /disposal, /craft.

MVP Rank

The MVP Rank has a chance of 6% in the crates. You can buy it in Gens for the VIP Rank and 5 Super compressed netherite. You have the benfits of the VIP Rank and you can write bold in chat and you can do /suicide, /heal, /enderchest.

Premium Rank

The chance for the Premium Rank at the crates is 3%. In Gens it costs 15 Super Compressed Netherite and the MVP Rank. With this rank you have all benefits of VIP and MVP and you can write in all styles in chat. You also have access to all tools with commands (/workbench, /grindstone, /anvil, etc.)

Supremium Rank

The Supremium Rank has a chance of 0.8% in crates. You can buy it with 32 Super Compressed Netherite and the Premium Rank. It has all benefits of the Premium Rank and you can set your personal time and weather (/ptime, /pweather).

Ultra Rank

The Ultra Rank has a chance of 0.16% in the crate. You can buy it with 64 Super Compressed Netherite in the Gens. You have the benefits of the Supremium Rank and you can fly in lobbies, you get 10.000 Mytstery Dust and you can do /hat, /seen and /strike.
